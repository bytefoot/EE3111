# EE3111: Statistics for Electrical Engineers
This repo contains our teams analysis and submisison for programming assignments of this course.
Each folder has it's notebook with the same name, a data folder and the problem statements as well as the in class component and questions.

## Overview
#### Lab 1: Random Walks & Quality Control
Visualisation of Law of Large Numbers (LLN), Central Limit Theorem (CLT), and Sampling Distributions.
- **Part A:** Casino's Edge simulation (Roulette).
- **Part B:** Quality Control sampling (Chi-Square check).
- **In-class:** Optimal betting strategies and statistic distribution identification.

#### Lab 2: Exploratory Data Analysis & Distribution Fitting
EDA on medical data and distribution fitting on survey data.
- **Part A:** Maternal Smoking EDA (Visual comparisons, Summary stats).
- **Part B:** Video Game Analytics (Gamma distribution fitting via MOM and MLE).
- **In-class:** CDFs, Violin plots, and Goodness of Fit metrics (Log-likelihood, KS test).

#### Lab 3: Bayesian Estimation
Application of Bayesian Inference with Conjugate and Non-conjugate priors.
- **Part A:** Titanic Survival (Beta-Binomial Model).
- **Part B:** Speed of Light estimation (Normal-Normal Model).
- **In-class:** Non-conjugate priors (Monte Carlo sampling) and Jeffreys Prior.

#### Lab 4: Hypothesis Testing with Real Data
Statistical testing on the Capital Bikeshare dataset.
- **Part A:** Working day vs. Non-working day analysis (t-tests, non-parametric tests).
- **Part B:** Demand vs. Weather independence (Chi-Square test).
- **In-class:** Temperature vs. Apparent Temperature analysis.

#### Lab 5: Hypothesis Testing with IPL Ball-by-Ball Data
Statistical analysis and hypothesis testing on cricket match data.
- **Part A:** Data organization (Parsing ball-by-ball and match info CSVs).
- **Part B:** Powerplay EDA (Scoring, boundaries, and wickets).
- **Part C:** Cricketing decisions (Two-proportion and Fisher exact tests for boundary/dismissal rates).
- **In-class:** Toss choices, IPL vs WPL score comparison, and Death over performance analysis.
